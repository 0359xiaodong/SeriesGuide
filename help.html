Hints and tips

For some hints and tips visit the developers website.

Known issues

After I change the time zone on my device the time and days are wrong

Please stop SeriesGuide and restart it. (Go into Settings >> Applications >> Manage Applications. Find SeriesGuide and stop it.)

SeriesGuide uses up more device memory than other Apps

See the FAQ about storing more data on the SD card below!

The app widget displays no data or is missing in the list of available widgets

This occurs if you move SeriesGuide to the SD card. For the widget to work, SeriesGuide has to be on your phone. Reboot afterwards, and everything should be fine again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app intended to play episodes, it will not do so on my device. What is wrong?

It is not possible to actually watch episodes through this app, sorry. SeriesGuide is just to keep track of your favorite TV shows: it displays episode information and let's you keep track of which episodes you already watched.


I want to use SeriesGuide in English or I think the translation is wrong/bad

All translations come from volunteers who translate free of charge (!) on crowdin. Please consider helping to translate or proof read! Now, to use the app in English you have to set your devices language to any variant of the English language.


Why can't I find show x or episode y? Why is information about show x or episode y wrong?

SeriesGuide gets its data from thetvdb.com, an open user-maintained tv database. If you want to help them you can add or edit show information and episodes yourself at thetvdb.com (make sure to read their guidelines first).


'No IMDB reference available. Update this show or go to thetvdb.com and add it.'

For sharing with GetGlue? a show in thetvdb.com needs to have an IMDBid set. This is typically the last part of its URL on imdb.com. If it is missing you can set it by logging into thetvdb.com and clicking the 'show/hide edit' on the shows overview page.
However some shows don't have IMDB entries, so currently it is not possible to share those via GetGlue? with SeriesGuide.


Why does SeriesGuide not store more data on the SD card?

Currently SeriesGuide only stores images on your SD card. Unfortunately this is not possible for the show database itself. This is due to limitations in the Android framework (Android decides where the database is stored) and to avoid wear of the SD card. Until a viable solution is found there is not much I can do right now. And I know your pain, I'm a Desire user.


Will a low number of downloads affect future releases of SeriesGuide?

I depend on this app myself, so for now the release frequency will only be affected by my student work.

Still lost?

If your problem can not be solved here, post your problem at the SeriesGuide Discussion Google Group. If you encountered a bug and know about bug reporting, please create a new issue.
