Known issues

GetGlue check-ins end up with the wrong show

Most of the time this means GetGlue has mapped a shows IMDb ID to the wrong show on their site. Just drop them a mail about it at support@getglue.com.
If you want to be sure, go to thetvdb.com, search for your show and look if the IMDb ID is correct (build a URL like http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0204993/).

I want to use SeriesGuide in English or I think the translation is wrong/bad

To use the app in English you have to set your devices language to any variant of the English language. Android will take care of the rest. By the way, all translations come from volunteers who translate free of charge (!) on crowdin. Please consider helping to translate or proof read!

After I change the time zone on my device the time and days are wrong

Please stop SeriesGuide and restart it. (From your home screen, go into Settings >> Applications >> Manage Applications. Find SeriesGuide and stop it.)

SeriesGuide uses up more device memory than other apps

You can put SeriesGuide on your SD card with the downside of disabling the app widgets. However, compared to other similar apps SeriesGuide uses a reasonable amount of memory. Keep in mind that all data is kept offline and indexed for fast search, this needs to be stored somewhere.
Currently SeriesGuide only stores images on your SD card. Unfortunately this is not possible for the show database itself. This is due to limitations in the Android framework (Android decides where the database is stored) and to avoid wear of the SD card.

The app widget displays no data or is missing in the list of available widgets

You may have moved SeriesGuide to the SD card. For the widget to work, SeriesGuide has to be on your phone or internal memory. Move it back and everything should be fine again (if not, try rebooting your device).

'No IMDb page found.'

This show is missing its IMDb ID entry on thetvdb.com required for viewing it in IMDb or sharing via GetGlue with SeriesGuide. The IMDb ID is the last part of its URL on imdb.com (e.g. tt0898266). You can add it by logging into thetvdb.com and clicking the 'show/hide edit' link on the shows overview page.
However keep in mind that some shows just don't have IMDb entries (yet?).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app intended to play episodes, it will not do so on my device. What is wrong?

It is not possible to actually watch episodes through this app, sorry. SeriesGuide just allows you to keep track of your favorite TV shows: it displays episode information and let's you quickly find out which episodes you have to watch next.

Why can't I find show x or episode y? Why is information about show x or episode y wrong?

First of all try updating (see here). Otherwise the information on thetvdb.com might be wrong or outdated. SeriesGuide gets its data from thetvdb.com, an open user-maintained tv database. If you want to help them you can add or edit show information and episodes yourself at thetvdb.com (make sure to read their guidelines first).

Still lost?

If your problem could not be solved here, post it at the SeriesGuide Discussion Google Group. If you encountered a bug and know about bug reporting, please create a new issue.
However, you can always just send an email via Send feedback in SeriesGuide or the Google play page.
