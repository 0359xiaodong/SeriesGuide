Getting Started

Overview video

The video below was shot on Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) with SeriesGuide version 2.2, so some things might look a little different on your device.

Keeping your shows updated

There are three options to update your shows:


Update outdated shows
Press the update icon (double arrows). This will update all shows which have not been updated for 7 or more days.

Update a single show
Sometimes you may want to get the latest info for just one show. To do this long-press on a show in the show list and select Update.

Update all shows
To get fresh data for every show, regardless when it has been updated last press the menu button on your device and select Update (all).

Backing up your show database

To backup your show database go into Settings and look for Backup/Restore. There you will find instructions on how to proceed.


Always have a 'backup' with trakt integration: Enable Integrate with trakt.tv in Settings. If you then mark an episode seen in a shows overview screen, it gets marked on trakt.tv, too. Now, to access your shows on another device just setup your trakt.tv account in Settings again and look for your shows in Add Show. After you added them you can sync the watched states from trakt.tv to your device via the Sync with trakt.tv setting. However this is still experimental so your milage may vary.
