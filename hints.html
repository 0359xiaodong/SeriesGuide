Getting Started

Set up trakt.tv sync

To quickly get started with trakt, follow these three steps:

  1. Look in Settings for trakt.tv to connect or create your trakt.tv account.

  2. Use the Sync to trakt.tv button to upload your existing shows to trakt.

  3. Add shows on other devices by swiping to the Library tab when adding shows.


Now whenever SeriesGuide updates your trakt activity is downloaded to all of your devices. This includes flagging episodes as watched or collected and check ins.

Check In, flag watched and collected

Each episode displays a button bar with four actions:

  • Check In (via GetGlue or trakt)

  • watched flag toggle

  • collected flag toggle

  • Create calendar event

Backup or restore your show database

To backup or restore your show database go into Settings and look for Backup/Restore. There you will find instructions on how to proceed.

Tip Some users sync their backup file with e.g. Dropbox around devices as an alternative to a trakt account.

Keep your shows up to date

You don't have to! If you make sure Auto Update is enabled in Settings, SeriesGuide updates every show at least every 7 days. However there are three options to manually update your shows:


Update outdated shows
Press the update icon (double arrows). This will update all shows which have not been updated for 7 or more days. Enabling Auto Update in Settings does the same thing on opening the show list.

Update a single show
Sometimes you may want to get the latest info for just one show. To do this long-press on a show in the show list and select Update.

Update all shows
To get fresh data for every show, regardless when it has been updated last, press the menu button on your device and select Update (all).

trakt.tv integration

If you want to know exactly what is going on with trakt.tv integration, read ahead!

Adding shows

Press the + button and then swipe to the Library tab.

Info SeriesGuide will try to load which episodes are already watched and collected from trakt when adding a show.


What is synced?

Check Ins
Submitted to trakt. On the next update checked-in episodes are marked as watched.

Watched flag
Changes are sent to trakt. Other SeriesGuide installations will mark episodes as watched, but never remove the watched flag on the next update.

Collected flag
Changes are sent to trakt. Other SeriesGuide installations will mark episodes as collected, but never remove the collected flag on the next update.

Info If you want to match up unwatched episodes, read the section below.


Bulk syncing

Sometimes you might be in need of syncing a bulk of watched flags, such as a whole season you just marked watched or to match up unwatched episodes with your trakt library.

Go into SeriesGuide Settings. Go to the trakt.tv section and look for Sync with trakt.tv. If you want to match up unwatched episodes, check the box on the bottom.
