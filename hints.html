Getting Started

trakt.tv integration

The following sections assume that you have connected your trakt account within SeriesGuide.
Look in Settings under trakt.tv if you want to.


Adding shows

Press the + button and then swipe to the Library tab. SeriesGuide will try to load which episodes are already watched from trakt.


What is synced?

Check Ins
Submitted to trakt. On the next update checked-in episodes are marked as watched.

Watched flag
Changes are sent to trakt. Other SeriesGuide installations will mark episodes as watched, but never remove the watched flag on the next update.

Collected flag
Changes are sent to trakt. Other SeriesGuide installations will mark episodes as collected, but never remove the collected flag on the next update.

Episode action bar: Check In button, watched flag and collected flag.

If you want to match up unseen episodes, read the section below.


Bulk syncing

Sometimes you might be in need of syncing a bulk of watched flags, such as a whole season you just marked watched or to match up unseen episodes with your trakt library.

Go into SeriesGuide Settings. Go to the trakt.tv section and look for Sync with trakt.tv. If you want to match up unseen episodes, check the box on the bottom.

Backup or restore your show database

To backup or restore your show database go into Settings and look for Backup/Restore. There you will find instructions on how to proceed.

Keep your shows up to date

You don't have to! If you make sure Auto Update is enabled in Settings, SeriesGuide updates every show every 7 days. However there are three options to manually update your shows:


Update outdated shows
Press the update icon (double arrows). This will update all shows which have not been updated for 7 or more days. Enabling Auto Update in Settings does the same thing on opening the show list.

Update a single show
Sometimes you may want to get the latest info for just one show. To do this long-press on a show in the show list and select Update.

Update all shows
To get fresh data for every show, regardless when it has been updated last, press the menu button on your device and select Update (all).
