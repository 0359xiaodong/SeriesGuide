Here's how it works



Add some shows

Tap the button to add shows. Search for a show name or swipe to the right to get trending shows on trakt.tv. Even access recommendations or your library after you setup your trakt account.



Filter your shows

Tap the filter on top to just display shows with unseen or upcoming episodes in the next 24 hours.



Watch and Check In

Check In what you are watching via trakt.tv or GetGlue. Or easily share it via Other Services through any supported Android app like Google+, Facebook and Twitter.



And much more...

Try adding the 'Next episodes' widget to your home screen. Look around in Settings. Explore!
