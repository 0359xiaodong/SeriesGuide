Here's how it works



Add some shows

Tap the button. Search for a show name or swipe to get trending shows on trakt.tv.
Even access recommendations or your library after you setup your trakt account.



Watch and Check In

Check in what you are watching via trakt.tv or GetGlue.
Or easily share an awesome episode with many installed Android apps like Google+, Facebook and Twitter.


Much more...

Add the Next episodes widget to your home screen. Customize SeriesGuide in Settings. Have a peek at the Getting Started page. Explore!
