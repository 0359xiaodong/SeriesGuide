Privacy Policy

Third-party tracking

SeriesGuide uses Google Analytics for Mobile and Crashlytics which may collect information about your device, log information (e.g. crash logs which might include show identifiers, check in messages and other information you entered) and used features and screens. SeriesGuide enables the 'Anonymize IP' flag for Google Analytics which tells Google Analytics to remove the last octet of your IPv4 address or the last 60 Bits of your IPv6 address prior to disk storage.

This data is used to maintain and improve SeriesGuide for you and others and help with the development of new features.

Detailed information about how Google handles the collected data can be found in their privacy policy and Google Analytics Privacy Overview. Information for Crashlytics can be found in their privacy policy.

Opt out of Google Analytics tracking

You may opt out of tracking:

  1. From the show list, swipe from the left edge to open the navigation drawer.
  2. Touch Settings.
  3. Touch Advanced.
  4. Touch Google Analytics to disable tracking.

SeriesGuide Cloud

SeriesGuide Cloud uses Google App Engine, it is covered by the Google privacy policy. If you sign in to SeriesGuide Cloud, the email address you used to sign will be stored, in addition to your SeriesGuide data (e.g. your shows, episodes, movies, statistics). You may remove your email address and data by removing your account on all devices connected to SeriesGuide Cloud. You can also send me an email, if you want me to purge your data.

Contact

If you contact me (e.g. via Email, Twitter, Facebook or Google+), I may keep a record of your communications to help solve any issues you might be facing. I may use your email address or contact data to inform you about SeriesGuide, such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements.

App permissions

What follows are explanations of why SeriesGuide requires certain permissions.

Use accounts on the device
Add or remove accounts
Create accounts and set passwords
Find accounts on the device
SeriesGuide creates a dummy account to enable integration with the Android sync framework and to store your trakt credentials. You can have a look at it in your Android account settings.

Google Play billing service
SeriesGuide has an in-app purchase to enable X features. This is handled through the Google Play billing service.

Full network access
SeriesGuide needs to download information from its data sources to display them inside the app: theTVDb.com, themoviedb.org. Also service integration with trakt and tvtag requires an internet connection.

Control Near Field Communication (NFC)
SeriesGuide allows you to beam a show to another device (open a shows overview, then touch another device with yours). This uses Android Beam which requires NFC.

Modify or delete the contents of your USB storage
Test access to protected storage
SeriesGuide caches images on your external storage. Also the backup and restore tool as well as Auto-Backup write and read their files from your external storage (USB/SD card/integrated).

View network connections
SeriesGuide has the option to only allow updates over WiFi and needs to behave well if you are offline. So it needs to check which, if any, connections are currently available.

Control vibration
SeriesGuide can show notifications, these can be set to also vibrate.

Prevent phone from sleeping
SeriesGuide listens when an extension is uninstalled or updated so it can remove or update it. While reacting to such a change, SeriesGuide needs to ensure your device does not go to sleep in the middle of it.

Run at startup
SeriesGuide can show notifications, so at startup it makes sure that the notification service is re-scheduled to wake up for the next one. This is delayed so it will not slow down your boot process.

Read sync statistics
Read sync settings
Toggle sync on and off
SeriesGuide integrates with the Android sync framework. It offers toggling SeriesGuide sync on and off inside its settings, needs to know if a sync is already running and needs to set it up on first run to sync periodically.

Install shortcuts
From a shows overview page you can create a shortcut and SeriesGuide needs this permission to automatically add it to your home screen.
