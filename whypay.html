Why pay and get SeriesGuide X?

Support development

SeriesGuide is developed as open source, non-paid. If you really like it, paying for it is a nice gesture showing your support. However you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features.

Get notifications

Favorite/Star shows to get notifications for upcoming episodes.

More list widget options

In addition to upcoming episodes, you can have a list widget (Android 3.0 and above) display recent episodes.

So what are you waiting for?


Get SeriesGuide X

FYI, to migrate your shows

With the Backup and Restore functionality in Settings you can easily transfer your show database to SeriesGuide X, or any other SeriesGuide installation for that matter.
