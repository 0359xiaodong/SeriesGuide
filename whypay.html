Why get X features?

Keep SeriesGuide alive

SeriesGuide is developed as open source and all but some convenience features are available for free. If you really like it, paying for it is a nice gesture showing your support for future features and fixes. However, you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features.

Get notifications

Favorite/Star shows to get notifications for upcoming episodes. Or go to settings to notify for all new episodes.

More list widget options

In addition to upcoming episodes, you can have a list widget (Android 3.0 and above) display recent episodes or your favorite shows and change its transparency.

Show shortcuts

Add shortcuts to your favorite shows to your home screen.

SeriesGuide X is now X Pass!

If you have already purchased SeriesGuide X or purchase the X Pass, install it side-by-side with SeriesGuide to unlock the X subscription permanently. No additional purchase necessary.
