Regular Updates and Features

SeriesGuide is developed as open source and all but some convenience features are available for free. If you really like it, paying for it is a nice gesture showing your support for future features and fixes. However, you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features.

Episode notifications

Get notifications for upcoming episodes.

More list widget options

In addition to upcoming episodes, you can have the list widget (Android 3.0 and up) display recent episodes or your favorite shows instead. And change its background transparency.

Additional Light and Dark Theme

Replace the default dark-purple theme with a light-purple or a "Holo dark"-blue variant.

Show shortcuts

Add a shortcut to the overview page of a show to your home screen.

Unlimited extensions

Enable an unlimited number of extensions. Extensions, once enabled, display quick actions below each episode.

SeriesGuide ships with an Amazon, Google Play, YouTube and Web search extension. Get third-party extensions in the Play store.
