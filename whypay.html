Why pay and get the X upgrade?

Support development

SeriesGuide is developed as open source, non-paid. If you really like it, paying for it is a nice gesture showing your support. However you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features.

Get notifications

Favorite/Star shows to get notifications for upcoming episodes. Or go to settings to notify for all new episodes.

More list widget options

In addition to upcoming episodes, you can have a list widget (Android 3.0 and above) display recent episodes and change its transparency.

SeriesGuide X users ride for free!

If you have already purchased SeriesGuide X, just install it side-by-side with SeriesGuide to unlock the X upgrade. No purchase necessary.

So what are you waiting for?


Get SeriesGuide

Once installed:

  1. From the show list, touch Menu .
  2. Touch Settings.
  3. Touch Services.
  4. Touch Get the X Upgrade and proceed with the purchase.

FYI, to migrate your shows

With the Backup and Restore functionality in Settings you can easily transfer your show database from SeriesGuide X, or any other SeriesGuide installation for that matter.
