What do you get?

Regular Updates and Features

SeriesGuide is developed as open source and all but some convenience features are available for free. If you really like it, paying for it is a nice gesture showing your support for future features and fixes. However, you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features.

Notifications

Favorite/Star shows to get notifications for upcoming episodes. Or go to settings to enable notifications for all new episodes.

More list widget options

In addition to upcoming episodes, you can have the list widget (Android 3.0 and above) display recent episodes or your favorite shows and change its transparency.

Additional Light and Dark Theme

Replace the default dark-purple theme with a light-purple or a "Holo dark"-blue variant.

Show shortcuts

Add shortcuts to your favorite shows to your home screen.

Customize and add more extensions

Enable more extensions beyond the free limit. Extensions, once enabled, display quick actions below each episode.
